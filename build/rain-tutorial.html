Step 5: Generate 'Project Rain' transaction(s)

Gridcoin

Command:
"sendmany {address:amount,...} [minconf=1] [comment]"

Example:
sendmany "1" '{"RzS1Z6vm84AiCJ8Nwe3V2JvvaQtWfnYfGf":112.322,"S9CDQg4yP9wuBA9N5PkbUv6T5b4JokrjMQ":68.939}' 5 "message"

Note: Amounts are double-precision floating point numbers & the wallet must be fully unlocked.

Bitshares

The general principle for generating, singing and broadcasting an arbitrary transactions:
1. Create an instance of the transaction builder
2. Add arbitrary operation types
3. Add the required amount of fees
4. Sign and broadcast your transaction
